About VitalNote

Health reporting you can actually use

VitalNote is an independent health and medical news publication. We cover nutrition, fitness, mental health, chronic disease, medical research and public health, with a simple standard for every story: is this actually useful to the person reading it, and is it accurately sourced?

We are not a substitute for medical advice, and we say so clearly on every article. What we aim to do instead is translate research and clinical guidance into something a reader can understand and act on, without the exaggeration that so much health content online relies on.

Editorial standards

  • Every article links to the studies or guidelines it draws on in a references section.
  • We distinguish clearly between findings that are well-established and findings that are preliminary or contested.
  • Articles are reviewed for clinical accuracy before publication.
  • We correct errors publicly and promptly when they're identified.
  • We do not accept payment in exchange for favorable coverage of any product, supplement, or treatment.

How we're funded

VitalNote is supported by display advertising. Advertising placements are clearly marked and do not influence our editorial coverage or recommendations.
