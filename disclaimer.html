Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Not a substitute for professional care

Nothing on VitalNote is, or is intended to be, medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified physician or other health provider with any questions you have about a medical condition, and never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you read on this site.

General information only

Articles on VitalNote are written for general educational purposes. They summarize research and public health guidance but cannot account for your individual medical history, current medications, or specific circumstances, all of which a qualified clinician needs in order to give you safe, personalized advice.

Emergencies

If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately or go to the nearest emergency department. Do not rely on this website for emergency medical guidance.

No doctor-patient relationship

Reading this site, subscribing to our newsletter, or submitting a comment or contact form does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and VitalNote, its writers, or its editors.

Accuracy and currency of information

We make a good-faith effort to keep articles accurate and up to date, and each article lists the sources it draws on. Medical understanding evolves, however, and an article's guidance may not reflect the most current research at the time you read it. We encourage you to verify any health decision with a current, qualified professional.

Where we link to outside studies, organizations, or websites, we do so for reference only. We do not control and are not responsible for the content or accuracy of external sites.

Supplements and medications

Any mention of a supplement, medication, or treatment on this site is descriptive, not a recommendation to use it. Speak with a pharmacist or doctor before starting, stopping, or combining any treatment.
